Frequently Asked Questions

You can track your order in real-time through the Yawigo app. Once your order is confirmed, you'll see live updates including restaurant preparation time and delivery progress on the map.

We accept Mobile Money (MTN, AirtelTigo, Vodafone), credit/debit cards, cash on delivery, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely with encryption.

To become a driver, you need a valid driver's license, vehicle registration, insurance, and smartphone. Apply through our website or visit our office for registration and vehicle inspection.

We currently serve Accra, Kumasi, Takoradi, Cape Coast, Tema, and Ho. We're rapidly expanding to more cities across Ghana. Check the app for service availability in your area.
